This document contains **all image and video generation prompts** for the LEOCON INFRA AAC Blocks website. Prompts are organized by page and include specifications for resolution, aspect ratio, and style guidelines.

## **Style Guidelines**

### **Brand Colors**

- **Primary:** `#ea580c` (burnt orange)
- **Secondary:** `#f8f7f4` (warm off-white cream)
- **Dark Accent:** `#1c1410` (warm charcoal)
- **Text:** Stone gray scale (400-900)

### **Photography Style**

- **Lighting:** Natural warm daylight, golden hour soft lighting for exteriors
- **Tone:** Professional, industrial, high-end construction industry aesthetic
- **Resolution:** 4K minimum for all images and videos
- **Format:** Wide-angle compositions for plant/facility shots, clean product shots for AAC blocks
- **Color Grading:** Warm tones, slight orange tint in highlights, avoid cold blues
